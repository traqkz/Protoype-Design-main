HOW TO PLAY

OBJECTIVE

Eat all the dots in the maze while avoiding the ghosts. Eat power pellets to turn the tables on the ghosts!

CONTROLS
  • ↑ Move Up
  • ↓ Move Down
  • ← Move Left
  • → Move Right
SCORING
  • • Regular dots: 10 points each
  • • Power pellets: 50 points each
  • • Ghosts (when vulnerable): 200, 400, 800, 1600 points
  • • Bonus fruits: 100-5000 points
